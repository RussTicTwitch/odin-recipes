Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

You're never too old for the classics. Enjoy this toasted and creamy grilled cheese.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese (or cheese of choice)

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat
  2. Butter one side of bread and place butter-side down into skillet
  3. Add 1 slice of cheese onto bread
  4. Butter second slice of bread and place on top of cheese butter-side up
  5. Grill until lightly browned and flip over; grill other side of sandwich
    • Repeat steps 1-5 for second sandwich
